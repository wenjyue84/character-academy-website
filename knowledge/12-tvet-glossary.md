# 12 — TVET Glossary (Malaysian Context)

A plain-English reference for the Malaysian TVET terminology that runs through Character Academy's work.

## JPK — Jabatan Pembangunan Kemahiran
The **Department of Skills Development** under the Ministry of Human Resources (KESUMA). JPK sets national occupational standards, accredits training centres (Pusat Bertauliah), appoints assessors and verifiers, and issues the national skills certificates.

## NOSS — National Occupational Skills Standard
*Standard Kemahiran Pekerjaan Kebangsaan.* The master document for every certified occupation in Malaysia. Specifies the competencies, tasks, knowledge, and skill progression required at each level. All SKM/DKM/DLKM curricula are anchored on the relevant NOSS.

## SKM / DKM / DLKM — The 5-tier Certification Framework

| Level | Name | Role tier | Typical duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Level 1** | SKM *(Sijil Kemahiran Malaysia)* | Operation / Production | ~6 months |
| **Level 2** | SKM | Operation / Production | ~6 months |
| **Level 3** | SKM | Supervisory | ~12 months |
| **Level 4** | **DKM** *(Diploma Kemahiran Malaysia)* | Executive | ~12 months + 3 months OJT |
| **Level 5** | **DLKM** *(Diploma Lanjutan Kemahiran Malaysia)* | Managerial | ~18 months |

Introduced in 2006. Three routes to certification: (a) training at a JPK-accredited centre, (b) National Dual Training System / ADI-Perantisan, (c) **PPT** (Pengiktirafan Pencapaian Terdahulu / Recognition of Prior Achievement) for experienced workers.

## ADI-Perantisan — Academy in Industry — Apprenticeship
The rebranded **National Dual Training System (NDTS / SLDN)**. Training splits **70–80% practical at the employer's site** and **20–30% theory at the training centre**. Apprentices earn an SKM. Host companies get HRD Corp levy reimbursement and can double-deduct under Section 34(6)(n) of the Income Tax Act 1967.

## Pusat Bertauliah
A JPK-**accredited training centre**. Only Pusat Bertauliah can deliver training leading to SKM / DKM / DLKM certification.

## PPL — Pegawai Pengesah Luaran
**External Verification Officer.** A JPK-appointed external auditor who visits Pusat Bertauliah, inspects assessment documents, witnesses final exams, and confirms *Kompeten / Belum Kompeten* status. Jennifer Tan has served in this role for 12+ years.

## Pakar Industri / Pakar Industri Negara (PIN)
**Country Industry Expert.** A senior industry practitioner appointed by JPK to advise on NOSS development, sit on examination panels (*Panel Hakim*), and verify industry relevance of training content. Jennifer Tan has been appointed in this role for 17+ years.

## HRD Corp — Human Resources Development Corporation
Runs Malaysia's mandatory HRD levy scheme under the **PSMB Act 2001**. Registered employers pay a 1% monthly levy and can claim it back when staff attend HRD Corp Claimable Courses delivered by accredited trainers.

## HRD Corp Accredited Trainer
An individual recognised to deliver HRD Corp claimable training. Must complete the 5-day **Train-the-Trainer (TTT)** programme (or qualify for exemption under Training Provider Circular 4/2009) and pass HRD Corp's accreditation-by-activity evaluation — totalling **120 training hours** of recognised competency.

## MTVET — National TVET Council
Established December 2020. Currently chaired by the Deputy Prime Minister under the MADANI administration. Coordinates TVET policy across ministries. Budget 2025–2026 allocated **RM7.9 billion** to strengthen the TVET ecosystem — which is the broader national tailwind that Character Academy rides.
