# 03 — Vision & Mission

## Vision

To become a leading force in Malaysia's TVET ecosystem — shaping a future where industry-driven education and structured skills development empower sustainable workforce growth. We pursue this through continuous industry collaboration, competency-based training, and nationally recognised certification pathways.

## Mission

1. Provide structured, **competency-based training** aligned with industry needs.
2. Support **workforce development** through systematic skills pathways.
3. Promote **national skills certification** (SKM / DKM / DLKM).
4. Develop competent **trainers, assessors, and industry practitioners**.
5. Strengthen **collaboration** between industry and training institutions.

## The one-liner

> "Adapting to and embracing change is key to long-term success."

## The guiding quote

> *"We understand that in today's ever-evolving world, adaptability is key. That's why at Character Academy, we embrace change as an opportunity for growth."*
