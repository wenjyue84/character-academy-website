# 01 — Company Snapshot

**Registered name:** Character International Academy Sdn. Bhd.
**Chinese name:** 名门全能美学职业培训学院 *(Mingmen All-Round Aesthetics Vocational Training Academy)*
**Also seen as:** 名门国际美学职业培训机构
**Founder & CEO:** Jennifer Tan
**Established:** circa 2002 (per Facebook handle `Character2002`)
**Industry:** TVET (Technical and Vocational Education and Training), with focus on beauty & aesthetics, corporate workforce development, and JPK-accredited skills certification.

## What they do — in one sentence

A JPK-accredited Malaysian TVET institution and corporate workforce-development consultancy that helps individuals earn SKM / DKM / DLKM national skills certificates and helps companies build structured, competency-based training systems.

## By the numbers

- **4,500+** learners certified under SKM / DKM / DLKM
- **80+** corporate partners in industry-based apprenticeship programmes
- **20+ years** of training experience
- **4 branches** across Johor and Negeri Sembilan

## Core offer in three lines

1. **For companies** — workforce development systems, apprenticeship implementation (ADI-Perantisan), HRD Corp claimable training, talent retention planning.
2. **For individuals** — SKM/DKM/DLKM certification pathways, upskilling advisory, HRD Corp trainer accreditation, JPK assessor/verifier advisory.
3. **For the industry** — acts as a "Power Team" hub connecting training providers, employers, and professionals into collaborative talent ecosystems.

## HQ

**No. 82, Jalan Ali, 84000 Muar, Johor** — with branches in Johor Bahru, Kluang, and Seremban.
