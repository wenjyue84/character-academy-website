# 07 — Project Case Studies

Three flagship initiatives that describe how Character Academy's approach shows up in practice.

## Project 1 — Industry-Based Apprenticeship Development

**80+ companies. One integrated model.**

Character Academy partnered with more than eighty employers to stand up **industry-based apprenticeship programmes** aligned with Malaysia's national TVET agenda. Workplace learning was woven together with competency-based classroom training so that apprentices developed skill and context at the same time.

**Outcomes at participating companies:**
- Measurably stronger workforce capability
- A repeatable, structured talent-development system
- Tighter operational consistency across teams
- Contribution to Malaysia's industry-driven skills ecosystem

## Project 2 — National Skills Certification Pathway (SKM / DKM / DLKM)

**4,500+ learners certified.**

This is Character Academy's largest and longest-running initiative: helping experienced workers obtain formal recognition of what they already know how to do. Through structured pathways — including Recognition of Prior Achievement (**PPT**) — workers across multiple sectors earned nationally recognised skills certificates under the JPK framework.

**Outcomes:**
- Improved employability and upward mobility for certified workers
- Formal credentials that match existing on-the-job competence
- Direct contribution to the national pool of certified skilled professionals
- Stronger supply of competency-based talent for Malaysian industry

## Project 3 — Corporate Workforce Development & Talent Retention System

**Fewer people quitting. More people growing.**

Character Academy has helped companies design and implement full **talent retention systems** — not just training, but the connective tissue around it: competency mapping, training matrix development, and certification-based career progression planning.

**Outcomes at participating companies:**
- Higher employee capability across the board
- Greater training consistency between teams and branches
- Measurably reduced turnover
- Stronger industry competitiveness, aligned with national TVET priorities
