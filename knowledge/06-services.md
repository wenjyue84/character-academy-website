# 06 — Services

Character Academy's offerings split cleanly into two audiences: organisations that want to build skilled, stable teams, and individuals who want formal recognition of their craft.

## Corporate Solution

Structured advisory and implementation services for organisations building sustainable workforce development systems.

| Service | What it is |
|---|---|
| **TVET Skills Training Programmes Development** | Custom competency-based training programmes mapped to NOSS and the SKM/DKM/DLKM framework |
| **ADI-Perantisan Industry Collaboration** | End-to-end setup and running of apprenticeship programmes under the National Dual Training System |
| **Accredited Training Centre Advisory** | Helping companies achieve and maintain Pusat Bertauliah accreditation with JPK |
| **Corporate Workforce Development Programmes** | Organisation-wide skills roadmaps, competency matrices, and certification pipelines |
| **Industry Competency-Based Training Implementation** | Hands-on advisory during rollout — not just a plan on paper |
| **Talent Retention Planning & Implementation** | Linking certification and career progression to retention outcomes |
| **Corporate Growth Planning & Advisory** | Talent strategy as a lever for business growth |
| **HRD Corp Claimable Training Provider** | Delivering training that employers can claim back under the HRD Corp levy scheme |

## Individual Development Solution

Pathways for professionals to build recognised credentials and advance their careers.

| Service | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Upskilling Advisory** | Diagnosing the skill gap and mapping the fastest credible path forward |
| **Certification Pathways Guidance** | Choosing the right SKM / DKM / DLKM level and the most practical route (training, ADI, or PPT recognition of prior learning) |
| **Competency Enhancement Guidance** | One-on-one advisory while you work toward assessment |
| **JPK Accreditation Personnel Advisory** | Guiding professionals who want to become assessors, verifiers (PPL), or industry experts (Pakar Industri) |
| **HRD Corp Accredited Trainer Advisory** | Helping trainers pass the 5-day TTT programme and the 120-hour accreditation process |
