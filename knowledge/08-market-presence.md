# 08 — Market Presence

Character Academy grows through networks, not broadcast marketing. Two repeatable processes sit underneath that growth.

## Process 1 — Industry Collaboration & Power Team Development

Character Academy builds **"Power Teams"** — clusters of industry partners, training providers, and professionals from complementary sectors that work together toward shared workforce-development goals. Instead of every organisation building its own training function from scratch, the Power Team model matches strengths and pools resources:

- Organisations contribute real workplace context and apprentice placements.
- Training providers contribute programme design and JPK-accredited delivery.
- Professionals and Pakar Industri contribute assessment and verification capability.

The result is a collaborative ecosystem that improves resource allocation, operational efficiency, and satisfaction across all partners.

## Process 2 — Certification-Driven Collaborative Talent Development

Every Power Team is tied to concrete outcomes — not meeting-minutes but national skills certificates. The workflow is:

1. **Competency identification** — what does the role actually need?
2. **Training implementation** — delivered jointly across workplace and training centre.
3. **Certification progression** — SKM Level 1 → 2 → 3 → DKM → DLKM, with each step opening a bigger role.

Because the process is shared across multiple partner organisations, knowledge travels between companies. Workers benefit from broader exposure; companies benefit from a deeper, more mobile talent pool; the wider ecosystem benefits from faster alignment with Malaysia's national TVET agenda.
