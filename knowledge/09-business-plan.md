# 09 — Business Plan

Three plans. One direction: make structured skills development more accessible, more specialised, and more internationally connected.

## Plan 01 — Digital TVET & Hybrid Learning Ecosystem *(Ongoing)*

Character Academy is building a **hybrid learning ecosystem** that combines physical workshops and on-site training with structured online support. The online layer handles pre-work, reference material, progress tracking, and remote coaching; the physical layer handles hands-on practice and assessment. The combination unlocks:

- **Scale** — regional reach without sacrificing assessment integrity.
- **Accessibility** — working professionals can progress without stepping away from their jobs.
- **Continuity** — competency development does not stop when the classroom empties.

## Plan 02 — Regional Centres of Excellence

Character Academy plans to stand up **specialised Centres of Excellence** focused on key industry sectors. Each Centre of Excellence will:

- Serve as a hub for **advanced skills development** (DKM Level 4, DLKM Level 5, specialist tracks).
- Run **trainer capacity-building** for HRD Corp Accredited Trainers and JPK assessors.
- Anchor **industry collaboration** at a sector level — not just a company level.
- Position Character Academy as a reference institution for its chosen verticals.

## Plan 03 — International Collaboration & Talent Mobility

Character Academy is developing partnerships with international training institutions and industry organisations to:

- Enable **knowledge exchange** and benchmark best practices.
- Raise **competency standards** to international levels.
- Open **cross-border opportunities** for certified professionals.
- Strengthen Malaysia's position in the **ASEAN Year of Skills** agenda and beyond.

> *"We understand that in today's ever-evolving world, adaptability is key. That's why at Character Academy, we embrace change as an opportunity for growth."*
