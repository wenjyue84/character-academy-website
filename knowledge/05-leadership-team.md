# 05 — Leadership Team

Founder of Character International Academy. Thirty years in corporate management and two decades leading JPK-accredited training work — with a track record that the Department of Skills Development has repeatedly drawn on as a public voice for Malaysian TVET.

**Timeline of roles and recognitions:**

- **30 years** in Corporate Management
- **24 years** as CEO & COO of Character Education Group of Academy
- **20 years** as Managing Director of True Harmony Group of Chain Company
- **17 years** appointed by JPK.gov.my as **Country Industry Expert (Pakar Industri)**
- **12 years** appointed by JPK.gov.my as **External Verification Officer (PPL — Pegawai Pengesah Luaran)**
- **10 years** accredited by JPK.gov.my as **Panel of Judges (Panel Hakim)**
- **5 years** **HRD Corp Accredited T-T-T** (Train-the-Trainer)
- **2022** — Success Story Speaker invited by JPK for the *"Institutionalise The Enterprise"* seminar
- **2023** — Success Story Speaker invited by JPK for the *"Advisory Service Program with Entrepreneurs"*
