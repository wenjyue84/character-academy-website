## 1. About Character Academy

Character International Academy Sdn. Bhd. is a professional Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) institution and corporate system development consultancy dedicated to national skills certification and structured workforce transformation. The academy focuses on nurturing highly competent professionals equipped with industry-relevant technical expertise and strong professional values.

Character Academy is committed to bridging industry needs with structured national skills frameworks. Through active industry engagement, the academy has established strategic partnerships with multiple companies to deliver industry-driven training programs and apprenticeship opportunities aligned with real workforce demands.

With more than two decades of training experience, Character Academy has successfully trained over **4,500 learners** to obtain National Certificate SKM/DKM/DLKM and collaborated with more than **80 corporate partners**. The academy continues to contribute to Malaysia's talent development by producing a highly skilled workforce with strong work ethics and professional competency.

## 2. Our History

Character International Academy Sdn. Bhd. was founded by an industry practitioner with multi-sector business and technical experience who recognised the need for structured skills development within organisations. Many businesses relied heavily on individual experience rather than systematic training, resulting in inconsistent performance and challenges in talent retention.

Driven by the mission to build sustainable workforce development, the academy began working closely with industry partners to introduce structured training pathways and competency-based learning approaches. These early initiatives focused on strengthening workforce capability while supporting organisational growth.

Over time, Character Academy expanded its role in supporting industry training needs and professional development across multiple sectors. Today, the academy continues to uphold its founding purpose by promoting structured skills development and contributing to long-term talent sustainability aligned with national standards.

## 4. How We Work — Our Structured Approach

At Character Academy, we adopt a systematic and industry-aligned approach to workforce development, ensuring that both organisations and individuals achieve measurable competency outcomes through structured pathways.

**Flow:** Needs → Standards → Programme → Implementation → Certification → Sustainability

1. **Industry & Organisational Needs Analysis** — Identify industry requirements and organisational gaps in skills, training systems, and workforce structure to ensure alignment with real operational needs.
2. **Standards Alignment** — Align with NOSS and national certification frameworks (SKM / DKM / DLKM).
3. **Programme Design** — Develop structured, competency-based training pathways.
4. **Implementation** — Deliver training through structured learning and workplace integration.
5. **Certification** — Facilitate competency assessment and recognised certification.
6. **Continuous Improvement** — Enhance training systems to ensure sustainability and talent retention.

## 7. Our Projects

### Project 1 — Industry-Based Apprenticeship Development
Character Academy has successfully collaborated with more than 80 companies to implement industry-based apprenticeship programmes aligned with Malaysia's national TVET agenda. By integrating workplace learning with competency-based training approaches, participating organisations achieved improved workforce capability and established structured talent development systems. This initiative strengthened internal skills development, enhanced operational consistency, and contributed to the growth of an industry-driven skills ecosystem in line with national workforce transformation policies.

### Project 2 — National Skills Certification Pathway (SKM / DKM / DLKM)
Character Academy has successfully supported more than **4,500 learners** in obtaining national skills certification under the SKM / DKM / DLKM framework. This project provided structured pathways for experienced workers to gain formal recognition of their competencies, improving employability and career progression. The initiative contributes directly to Malaysia's TVET agenda by expanding the pool of certified skilled professionals and supporting industry demand for competency-based talent.

### Project 3 — Corporate Workforce Development & Talent Retention System
Character Academy has successfully assisted multiple organisations in implementing structured workforce development and talent retention systems. Through competency mapping, training matrix development, and certification-based career progression planning, participating companies achieved improved employee capability, greater training consistency, and reduced workforce turnover. This project aligns with national TVET priorities by supporting sustainable talent development and strengthening industry competitiveness.

## 9. Our Business Plan

### Plan 01 — Digital TVET & Hybrid Learning Ecosystem *(Ongoing)*
Character Academy is implementing a digital-enabled TVET ecosystem that integrates hybrid learning by combining physical training with structured online support. This initiative enhances accessibility, supports continuous competency development, and enables scalable workforce training with structured progress tracking across regions.

### Plan 02 — Regional Centre of Excellence Development
Character Academy plans to establish specialised Centres of Excellence focused on key industry sectors. These centres will serve as hubs for advanced skills development, trainer capacity building, and industry collaboration, strengthening sector-specific competencies and positioning the academy as a reference institution.

### Plan 03 — International Collaboration & Talent Mobility
Character Academy aims to develop international partnerships with training institutions and industry organisations to promote knowledge exchange, benchmark best practices, and enhance competency standards. This initiative also expands opportunities for certified professionals and strengthens global workforce competitiveness.
